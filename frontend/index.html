Global Climate Summit Reaches Historic Agreement on Carbon Reduction

World leaders commit to ambitious new targets as scientists warn of critical tipping points

By Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen

The new framework establishes binding commitments for developed nations to achieve net-zero emissions by 2035, five years earlier than previous targets.
